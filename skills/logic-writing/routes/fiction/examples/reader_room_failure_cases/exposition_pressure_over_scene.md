# Failure Type
exposition_pressure_over_scene

# Bad Excerpt
The argument proved that Mira feared intimacy more than loneliness, and that the whole expedition had become a test of whether she could accept trust.

# Why It Fails
The narrator explains the scene's meaning instead of making the reader infer it from action, dialogue, silence, contradiction, or consequence.

# Better Direction
Let Mira fold the spare blanket without offering it, leave the lantern between herself and the other traveler, or answer a confession with a practical question.

# Route
Return to prose-native projection, scene contract, and voice/style exposition policy.
