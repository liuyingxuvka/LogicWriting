# Failure Type
same_voice_cast

# Bad Pattern
The student, the grandmother, the captain, and the shopkeeper all speak in clean thematic summaries about duty, memory, and sacrifice. Their sentences differ in content but not in pressure, evasion, status, or knowledge boundary.

# Why It Fails
The cast collapses into one author-summary voice. Shared topic is allowed; shared abstract delivery without character-specific pressure is voice/style drift.

# Better Direction
Give each major character a voice source: rank, fear, habit, self-protection, what they refuse to say, what they notice first, and how they manage risk.

# Route
Return to character voice distinction in voice/style continuity.
