# Failure Type
late_payoff_without_prior_support

# Bad Pattern
The final reconciliation depends on a childhood song that has not appeared, echoed, or affected a prior scene. It arrives as the key emotional proof only in the last chapter.

# Why It Fails
The payoff may be moving, but the final artifact does not support it with earlier continuity or promise evidence. It feels introduced for the ending rather than recovered by the ending.

# Better Direction
Add earlier low-emphasis support: a hummed fragment, a character flinching at the melody, a wrong lyric, or a silence around the song.

# Route
Return to promises, continuity rows, and earlier prose if needed.
