# Failure Type
premature_reveal_without_support

# Bad Excerpt
Before the apprentice even touched the broken compass, she understood that the map was lying because the mountain had moved.

# Why It Fails
The prose states the mechanism before the reader has enough scene evidence to participate. It may be true in the model, but the reader-room sequence is too early.

# Better Direction
Show the compass failing, the shadow falling the wrong way, or the mentor refusing to name the problem. Let the category of explanation arrive after the anomaly is felt.

# Route
Return to promise/payoff timing, reader-state order, and prose projection.
