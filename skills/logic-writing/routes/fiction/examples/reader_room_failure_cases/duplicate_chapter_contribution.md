# Failure Type
duplicate_chapter_contribution

# Bad Pattern
Three consecutive chapters each show one more neighbor saying the family secret was already known. None adds cost, contradiction, changed relationship pressure, or a new reader interpretation.

# Why It Fails
The actual prose repeats the same contribution: confirmation that the secret was known. The chapters may contain different events, but the reader-state movement is equivalent.

# Better Direction
Merge the confirmations, or change one chapter into a cost, a contradiction, a betrayal, a failed denial, or a consequence that makes the known secret matter differently.

# Route
Return to post-draft contribution review and chapter interface.
