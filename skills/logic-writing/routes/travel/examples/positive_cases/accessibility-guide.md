# Step-free city guide

## Day 1 — Step-free arrival

Use the central hotel as the luggage base and confirm the step-free main station exit before starting. The design shop street is kept on the accessible side of the route, with a quiet cafe rest nearby. If a lift outage or fatigue becomes the trigger, skip the shop and return to the hotel before the local bistro dinner.

## Day 2 — Accessible weather branch

Confirm lifts, entrances, and accessible toilets before dinner. Use the riverside viewpoint only when its step-free access and weather exposure are acceptable; otherwise switch to the indoor gallery as the named fallback. The hotel-area cafe remains a second fallback for rain, wind, crowding, or an access failure.

## Day 3 — Accessible departure buffer

Stay near the central hotel for a cafe rest and one souvenir stop, then use the confirmed step-free station route. Climate evidence licenses only an initial plan, so forecast, alerts, transport access, opening hours, reservations, and prices require a current recheck.
