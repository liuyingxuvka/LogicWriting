# Low-stamina city guide

## Day 1 — Short arrival loop

Begin at the central hotel, leave luggage there, and use the main station only as a nearby arrival anchor. The design shop street remains optional and short; if fatigue appears, take the quiet cafe rest instead. A local bistro dinner stays close to the hotel, so the first day never depends on a long late transfer.

## Day 2 — Comfort route first

Use the comfort route as the default, with no more than a short walk between dinner and the indoor gallery. If rain, strong wind, crowding, or low energy becomes the trigger, the hotel-area cafe is the named fallback. The riverside viewpoint is optional and must be dropped before the rest buffer is consumed.

## Day 3 — Protected departure

Keep the departure morning beside the central hotel with a cafe rest and one small souvenir stop. The climate evidence remains a planning boundary; forecast and alert details require a later recheck, and the initial plan does not prove current opening hours, reservations, transport, or price.
