# Family city guide

## Day 1 — Child-protected arrival

Start at the central hotel and store luggage before using the main station as the arrival anchor. The family takes a short design shop street segment, with a quiet cafe rest ready when the child shows fatigue. Keep the local bistro dinner early, avoid a late long transfer, and return to the hotel before energy drops.

## Day 2 — One peak with a clear exit

Choose one evening peak: the riverside viewpoint in comfortable weather or the indoor gallery when rain, wind, queueing, or child fatigue becomes the trigger. The gallery is the named nearby fallback, while the hotel-area cafe protects a second exit. A small souvenir stop can stay only if it does not displace food or rest.

## Day 3 — Gentle family close

Remain near the central hotel for a cafe rest, a market snack, and one final souvenir stop. The climate source supports only an initial planning shape, so current forecast, alerts, opening hours, reservations, price, transit, and family-safety details must be rechecked before travel.
