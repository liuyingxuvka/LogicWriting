# SourceGuard adapter

SourceGuard owns the next-search/action policy and evidence-discovery depth.
Logic Writing supplies the claim contract, source roles, existing observations,
access constraints, and stopping needs; it consumes SourceGuard's native result
without copying its planner.

Preflight the real provider on every run. Do not persist its machine-specific
import path. Preserve `candidate`, `observed`, `access_gap`, `bounded`,
`planning_only`, `provider_unavailable`, and native receipt status exactly.
Only observed source content with resolvable lineage may enter factual support.
